Our Story

Where Every Bite Tells a Story

What began as a small kitchen on Hoshangabad Road in 2015 has grown — quietly, steadily — into a name that families across Bhopal return to, generation after generation.

Founded by Mohammed Ikram, Ayesha Biryani House was built on four simple values that have never changed: authentic flavour, uncompromising cleanliness, respectful service, and honest dealing.
