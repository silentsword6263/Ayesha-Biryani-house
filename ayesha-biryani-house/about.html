Our Story

A Decade of Honest Cooking

Ayesha Biryani House has been serving Bhopal for over a decade. What began as a small kitchen on Hoshangabad Road has grown — quietly, steadily — into a name that families across the city return to, generation after generation.

Founded by Mohammed Ikram in 2015, the restaurant was built on four simple values that have never changed: authentic flavour, uncompromising cleanliness, respectful service, and honest dealing.

Every biryani that leaves our kitchen is cooked the way it was meant to be — with patience, with care, and with the kind of attention you'd expect from your own home.

What We Believe

Built on Trust

We believe a good meal isn't just about taste. It's about trust. Trust that the food is halal, trust that the kitchen is clean, and trust that the people serving you genuinely want you to leave happier than you came in.

Over ten years, we have watched children grow up on our biryani. We have fed families during celebrations, supplied food for gatherings, and welcomed strangers who became regulars. That is not something you can build through marketing. It is something you earn, one plate at a time.

Hoshangabad Road has changed a great deal since 2016. New restaurants have come and gone. We are still here — same address, same flavours, same faces behind the counter.

Welcome to Ayesha Biryani House. Khaana ho toh aisa.

— Mohammed Ikram, Founder

What Drives Us

Our Four Values

The same principles Mohammed Ikram built this kitchen on in 2015. Nothing has changed.

Authentic Flavour

No shortcuts. Every recipe follows the way it was meant to be made — whole spices, proper technique, real ingredients.

Uncompromising Cleanliness

A clean kitchen is not optional. We hold ourselves to the standard we'd expect if we were eating here ourselves.

Respectful Service

Every customer is treated like a guest in our home. Simple, warm, and without pretension.

Honest Dealing

Fair prices, accurate portions, and no hidden surprises. What you see on the menu is what you get on the plate.

Our Commitment

All Meat Sourced Halal

All meat used at Ayesha Biryani House is sourced halal. This is not a marketing claim — it is a commitment we make to our customers and our community. We do not compromise on this, and we never will.
